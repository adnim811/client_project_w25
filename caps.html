Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

About CAPS

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) provides confidential psychological services for currently enrolled U of M undergraduate and graduate students. CAPS offers a safe and supportive environment based on its multicultural, multi-disciplinary, and multi-theoretical values and practices.

Services Offered

  • Individual and Relationship Counseling
  • Groups, Workshops, and Lunch Series
  • Teletherapy Services
  • Trauma-Informed Support
  • Gender-Affirming Services
  • Crisis Services
  • Care Management and Referrals

Making an Appointment

You can schedule an appointment with CAPS by:

  • Visiting the CAPS office at the Michigan Union
  • Calling (734) 764-8312
  • Scheduling online through the CAPS website

For detailed steps, visit the CAPS Scheduling Page.
